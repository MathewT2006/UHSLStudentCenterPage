Frequently Asked Questions

The UHSL Student Center is open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Saturday/Sunday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Hours are subject to change during university holidays and semester breaks; please check the Contact page for the most current information.

Visitor parking is available in Lot 3, directly accessible from University Drive. Valid UH parking permits are required for all non-visitor lots. Guests attending events should follow event-specific parking instructions provided by the host.

Study Pods on the second floor can be booked for up to 3 hours per day by current UH students. Conference Rooms require approval and are generally reserved for faculty, staff, and large student organizations. All reservations must be made via the online booking form found in the "Rooms & Booking" section. Study Pods on the first floor are intended to be walk-in.

Yes, covered drinks and light snacks are generally permitted in all areas, including the Study Pods and Student Hub. Full meals and catering are only permitted in the Campus Café and designated Conference Rooms. Please dispose of all trash properly.

Lost and found items are managed at the Information/Reception desk located in the main lobby on Floor 1. Please visit the desk or call the main number on the Contact page to report or inquire about a lost item.
